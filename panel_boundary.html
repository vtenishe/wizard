Simulation Domain Boundary
Outer boundary where SEPs are injected and escaping particles are terminated ‚Äî rectangular box (GSM) or Shue (1998) magnetopause surface
NEW ¬∑ Step 4 ‚ñº
Physical role: AMPS traces SEP and GCR particles backward in time from the output location to the outer boundary. Particles that reach the outer boundary are assigned the source spectrum (Panel 6). Particles that mirror and return inward without reaching the boundary are classified as trapped or forbidden. A tighter, physics-based boundary (Shue) reduces wasted computation beyond the real magnetopause, while a simple box is easier to reason about and reproduces the legacy AMPS behaviour. Maps to DOMAIN_BOUNDARY block in AMPS_PARAM.in.

Rectangular Box (GSM)
Axis-aligned cuboid defined by six faces in GSM coordinates (Earth radii RE). Simple geometry; identical in all solar wind conditions; reproduces legacy AMPS 2016 default domain. Recommended for GCR runs and parameter sweeps.
BOUNDARY_TYPE = BOX
