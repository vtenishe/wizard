Electric Field Model
Magnetospheric electric fields that guide particle drift orbits. Maps to #ELECTRIC_FIELD in AMPS_PARAM.in.
NEW ¬∑ Step 5 ‚ñæ
Why electric fields matter: During storms the dawn-dusk convection electric field penetrates deep into the inner magnetosphere, disrupting radiation belt access and modifying particle drift paths. Including a realistic E-field is essential for storm-time cutoff rigidity calculations. The Volland‚ÄìStern model is the robust default; Weimer adds IMF/solar-wind sensitivity for event-specific realism.
A ‚Äî Corotation Electric Field

The corotation electric field E = ‚àí(œâ√ór)√óB maintains particles co-rotating with Earth inside the plasmasphere (L < 4‚Äì5). Disabling it is physically incorrect for trapped radiation-belt particles. Strongly recommended: leave ON.
